Planwise Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

Planwise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our AI-powered productivity app for managing tasks, habits, goals, and schedules.

Information We Collect

When you use Planwise, the following data may be processed:

  • Tasks, Habits, Goals, and Events: The content you create within the app is stored locally on your device and synced via iCloud to your other devices.
  • AI Conversations: When you use the AI assistant, your messages and relevant context (schedule, tasks, habits, goals) are sent to our servers for processing.
  • Voice Input: If you use voice features, audio is processed using Apple's Speech Recognition framework on-device.
  • Photos and Attachments: Files you attach to tasks are stored locally and in iCloud.
  • Crash Data: Anonymous crash reports to help us improve the app (opt-in).

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered scheduling and productivity assistance
  • To sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To process subscription payments through Apple

AI Processing

When you chat with our AI assistant:

  • Your messages and relevant app data are sent to our secure server
  • We use OpenAI's API to process requests
  • We do not permanently store your conversation history on our servers
  • OpenAI processes data according to their Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Local Storage: Your tasks, habits, goals, and calendar data are stored locally on your device using Core Data.

iCloud Sync: If enabled, your data syncs across devices using Apple's CloudKit, governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Attachments: Files and photos you attach are stored locally and in iCloud.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API: For AI-powered assistance
  • Apple CloudKit: For data synchronization
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management (no payment data stored by us)
  • Apple Speech Recognition: For voice input (processed on-device)

Data Security

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Your data is encrypted on your device
  • iCloud sync uses Apple's encryption standards
  • We do not have access to your Apple ID or payment information

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Disable iCloud sync in Settings to stop cloud storage
  • Disable AI features if you prefer not to use them
  • Disable crash reporting in app settings
  • Request data deletion by contacting us

Children's Privacy

Planwise is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
